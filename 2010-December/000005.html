> And it does also seem like my mails do not pass to an ML...
> Whats going on?

I don't know about the Berlios mailing list, but I'm using graylisting 
as an antispam filter for my personal mail at thyrsus.com.  It means that
the first (and *only* the first) mail attempt from any new address/IP
combination is rejected, but retries are accepted.  This exploits the
fact that many spamcasters do not retry.

So your mail should bve through to me once he IP/address pair from the
first try is in my graylist database.
